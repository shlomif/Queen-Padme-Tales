<s id="about-objective" title="Objective">

[

An illustrated screenplay crossing <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars%3A_Episode_I_%E2%80%93_The_Phantom_Menace">Star Wars Ep. I</a>,
the <a href="https://buffyfanfiction.fandom.com/wiki/Selinaverse">Selinaverse</a> (itself
crossing Star Trek TNG/DS9, Buffy, Judaism, Israel, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Objectivism_(Ayn_Rand)">Objectivism</a>, etc.)
the real world online/offline life in 2010s/2020s,
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wayne%27s_World_(film)">Wayne's World</a>,
and
<a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/My_Little_Pony_Friendship_is_Magic">My Little Pony</a>.

It aims to launch the "Queen Padmé Tales" web series (
whose format is
modelled loosely after <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ox_Tales">Ox Tales</a>
). It stars <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiffany_Alvord">Tiffany Alvord</a> as
<a href="https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Padm%C3%A9_Amidala">Queen Padmé Amidala of the Naboo</a>, and which
aims to be codirected and costarred by
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natalie_Portman_filmography">Natalie Portman</a>.
More ambitiously it aims to pave way for commercial crossover / <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_person_fiction">RPF</a> fanart, help reverse copyright
maximalism and convert Hollywood and the film industry at large to the open/free/amateur model ( see <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/fortunes/show.cgi?id=sharp-cinema--copyright-cannibalism">Copyright Cannibalism</a> ).

We may not succeed, but at least we're going to try.

This screenplay <b>is not</b> written in the Hollywood blessed format because
<a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/philosophy/philosophy/putting-cards-on-the-table-2019-2020/putting-cards-on-the-table-2019-2020/hacking-heroism.xhtml">good
hackers (= resourceful and rule bending heroes)</a>
which include the talented actors and actresses in this film can
withstand reading a raw and non-CSS-styled XHTML5 file. That - and hackers like
me do not have the time to massage a screenplay into Hollywood's whimsical
format only to be rejected, rinse and repeat.

]

</s>

<s id="dedication" title="Dedication">

[

The <b>Dedication</b>:
<img src="images/Christina_Grimmie_in_November_2014--resized.webp" alt="Photo of Christina Grimmie" />

This story is dedicated to the memory of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christina_Grimmie">Christina Grimmie (1994-2016)</a>,
a remarkable singer and youtuber, who was killed at age 22 by a fan who was
obsessed with her, who almost immediately committed suicide himself.

For what I consider her Magnus Opus see her original
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kYX8sjIzjGw">"Feelin' Good" song and videoclip</a>
whose message I believe is:

«

Be confident. Do the best you can given the time frame.
It doesn't have to be perfect. You are allowed to be wrong and say wrong things
that you think will still impress people. Try to learn from your mistakes,
<a href="http://shlomifishswiki.branchable.com/Encourage_criticism_and_try_to_get_offended/">encourage the critics</a>
and try to improve, but realise that
<a href="http://shlomifishswiki.branchable.com/Never_Try_to_Please_Everyone/">some people will always be unhappy</a>
and hold you liable for your past opinions, past mistakes,
past failures, opinions that they disagree with, your non-normative behaviour,
your qualities (age, gender, country, city, ethnicity, religion, ideology,
beliefs, wealth, image, personality, cultural tastes, etc.) and your works.

You will likely "fail" to become the "next biggest thing", but even if you
do, you can at least fail "in style" and inspire or help even just <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/fortunes/show.cgi?id=mishnah--saving-one-soul">one person</a>.

Always remember: you are awesome. You can become more awesome, regardless
of any "IQ" myths. But you may one day <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Summerschool-at-the-NSA/ongoing-text.html#summer_glau_presents__the_talk__amateur_fighters">"lose"</a> to someone less qualified than you.
That's OK - <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Buffy/A-Few-Good-Slayers/ongoing-text.html#becky_in_the_library__chit_chat">you can learn from a lost fight</a> and make
<a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/philosophy/culture/my-real-person-fan-fiction/#actors_are_unique">a comeback</a>.
Frankly, heroes do not die ("reputationally" at least) - they accumulate.

As the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kQKrmDLvijo">Indiana Jones' gun vs, sword</a>
scene shows, if something takes too long or seems too risky, then think outside
the box, challenge the invisible rules, "hack" something, or even temporarily
or permanently give
up. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/There%27s_more_than_one_way_to_do_it">There's more than one way to do it</a>
(even <a href="https://brilliant.org/wiki/proofs-of-the-pythagorean-theorem/">in maths</a> and
when writing <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Python_%28programming_language%29">Python</a> code) and different people like different things.

»

(Also see <a href="https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/46473/if---">If—</a> by Rudyard
Kipling which has a similar message, and is the most favourite poem among
British citizens, a favourite among Israelis, was Ayn Rand’s favourite, and mine.)

]

<s id="ethical--peaceful-resolution" title="Peaceful Resolution">

[ The filming set. ]

Natalie: everyone in position…and - action!

[ Worf and Gowron growl. The two closest Jedi knights quickly pull
two <a href="https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/DL-44_heavy_blaster_pistol">black blasters</a> and aim them at the Klingons; Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon do the same. ]

The Jedi Knights: hands up!

[ The Klingons comply. ]

Queen Padme: [using her smartphone as a loud speaker.] Dear Klingon sirs…
what did you intend to do?

Worf: we were hired to kill you and our plan has failed. We thought using
missiles would be too cowardly.

+++: Nevertheless…it is against
the Klingon ethos to retreat from a battle nonvictorious, so please
kill us now, and if you wish, torture us beforehand.

[ Worf and Gowron close their eyes. Padmé sighs.]

Padmé: OK… let's suppose for the sake of argumentation that my jedi knights
and I have killed you (or <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Selina-Mandrake/">slain</a>
you), that you have died (or admitted you were wrong), and were reborn.
Will this be acceptable?

[ Worf and Gowron smile, then laugh, open their eyes and are
relieved. ]

Gowron: you were a truly worthy opponent your majesty!
What should we do next?

Padmé: [uses her smartphone. Shows Darth Vader on the screen]
Hey uncle Vader! Guess what? We found the assassins and they
have been reborn. We're going to chat with them and get some
"intel" out about their clients. But tell aunt Liz to be
the ceremonial female royal in the Jedi tournament instead
of me.

Vader: sure thing, Padpad! May the force be with you.

Padmé: [to Worf and Gowron] OK, you seem to be noble and think highly
of me now. What prompted you to attempt to assassinate me?

Worf: we accumulated debt… wine, song and the wrong kind of women.
So Gowron and I started a side-business as mercenaries. We
were offered a large amount of money to assassinate you,
which we accepted after reading your Wikipedia page,
which gave us the impression that you were some kind of power,
fame, and money hungry politician, tyrant, and celebrity.

Padmé: A power, fame, and money hungry politician, tyrant, and celebrity?
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KNYI3iINXrQ">Why, thank you!</a>

[ They laugh. ]

Padmé: OK, seriously now: I had my share of frustrations from
that page, which is kind of a "can't see the forest from the trees"
syndrome, and being a royal doesn't help (and I'll gladly pass the
crown to someone I can trust, but nobody volunteered so far.)

+++: Anyway, Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon will escort you to the palace's
café where you can find an open wifi, some tablets, a screen,
and a digital video camera, and can order <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/philosophy/philosophy/putting-cards-on-the-table-2019-2020/#amateur-modelled-commerce">free food</a>.

+++: Please give them the identity of your client - both for my good,
as I have likely been doing something wrong, and for your client's.

+++: Once you arrive at the café, I suggest you start reading
my bio on my personal website and my FAQ.

[ Rainbow Dash and <a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Big_McIntosh">Big Mac</a>
materialise out of thin air carrying large blasters. ]

Rainbow Dash: We'll make sure they do not leave the palace's premises!

Big Mac: ayyup!

Padmé: Oh Lord! Are you trying to scare people though?

Gowron: Rainbow, Big Mac: to quote
<a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Kahless_the_Unforgettable">Kahless
the Unforgettable</a>:
“Only the most timid of warriors shall attempt to evade an opportunity of
peaceful enlightenment.”. We have no intention to escape, and surely you can
outrun… or outfly us.

[ <b>Note:</b> I (= Shlomi Fish) made up this Kahless quote. ]

Rainbow Dash: Fair enough, <a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Discord">Discord</a>,
please take care of these blasters.

[ Discord appears, snaps his fingers and converts Rainbow Dash's and Big Mac's blasters
into a <a href="https://duckduckgo.com/?q=mirror+dice&amp;atb=v140-1&amp;iax=images&amp;ia=images">Mirror Dice</a>
-like ornament. ]

Discord: These will look great on my new <a href="https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Podracer">podracer</a>.

[ He flies in a podracer not unlike the Star Wars Ep. 1's Anakin Skywalker's podracer
, with scarves and sunglasses
similar to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thelma_%26_Louise">Thelma &amp; Louise</a>, does a U-turn,
and leaves the frame. ]

Padmé: Well, I need to go to my room and change. This ceremonial robe is
itchy. I'm going to need one of you Jedi knights' gentlemen to escort
me. I have my own blaster here, but still will need a body guard in
the unlikely case that there are going to be more assassins.

<s id="at-the-cafe" title="At the café">

[

Worf, Gowron, Obi-Wan, Qui-Gon, Big Mac and Rainbow Dash are at the
café. Worf and Gowron are reading Padmé’s FAQ on tablets and are laughing
and discussing it with the others. Worf is wearing glasses.

Padmé approaches them wearing a captioned T-shirt, trousers, and

[ He walks away. Padmé sits down. ]

Padmé: hi, sorry it took me so long - I have too much to wear…

Worf: hah! I thought I'll never hear a lady say that!

Padmé: yes, well: I have this offer where people can ask me to buy one T-shirt/etc.
for them and one for myself if I like the design in their link. And I often do,
and I hate to disappoint them, so…

Worf: well, frankly you look both more dashing and more authoritative
now than in that ridiculous ceremonial robe! Heh…

Gowron: I agree!

Padmé: thank you! On the other hand, most Jedis (both men and women)
like their uniforms… misery!

Gowron: really? How so?

Qui-Gon: well, they are comfortable, and fairly functional, and have a cool
retro look… and they sort of convey authority.

Obi-Wan: Chicks love them too!

[They laugh.]

Worf: How many girls do you need?

Obi-Wan: <a href="https://quoteinvestigator.com/2014/07/16/bigamy/">One too many</a>

Qui-Gon: I suggest you <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fifth_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution">take the fifth</a>!

Obi-Wan: you think I have five girlfriends?

Gowron: Or more.

[ They laugh. ]

Worf: Anyway… back to business: your majesty, your FAQ is incredibly funny.

Padmé: yes, well… it is mostly written seriously. [She seems unhappy.]

Worf: well, its <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/quotes/49156-comedy-is-simply-a-funny-way-of-being-serious">comedic effect may indicate it is more true</a>.

Gowron: Well, we jointly owe Robandy of the Orion syndicate 220 thousand
dollars. He erased our bet deficit at <a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Quark%27s_Bar,_Grill,_Gaming_House_and_Holosuite_Arcade">Quark’s</a>

Padmé: 220,000 USD? Are you kidding? It is pocket change for me. I can SWIFT
you 3 millions dollars to erase the bet, and have a fresh start.

Worf: As a loan?

Padmé: Nah… as a present. I dislike keeping track of loans
<a href="https://shlomifish.livejournal.com/1991.html">in my head</a>.
These things
<a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/fortunes/show.cgi?id=smg-about-giving-back-money-and-time">tend to bounce back</a>
anyway (unfortunately for me and my increasing bank balance).

+++: Please promise that you'll try to avoid getting drunk in the future,
though. There are fruit juices, there are carbonated beverages, there are
syrups… teas and infusions… malt beers… they may cloud your judgement a bit,
but not enough to go into debt.

Gowron: Thank you, your majesty! This is especially noble of you given we
just tried to kill you. But following <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Selina-Mandrake/ongoing-text.html#from-dispeller-to-selina">his return</a>,
Kahless did quote other fine warriors that "an eye for an eye will make the
whole world blind".

+++: I've emailed you our SWIFT details.

</s>

<s id="padmes-frustrations-with-her-life" title="Padmé’s Frustrations With her Life">

Padmé: I should note that I was less lucky being unlucky at Quark’s.

Obi-Wan: you mean… you tried to lose money?

Padmé: Yeah… first I tried to make the most atrociously atrocious predictions
at his "Stock Exchange of The Profiles" and… most of them materialised.

[ Obi-Wan smiles. ]

Padmé: Then I tried
<a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Dabo">Dabo</a>, a game of pure
chance as the computer cannot read my mind, am I right? I tried to lose, and…
I've won.

Gowron: Sounds like you have <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midas#Golden_Touch">Midas' Golden Touch</a>, your majesty!

Padmé: Yes, only at this rate, I'll turn my firstborns into gold too!

Gowron and Worf: hah hah… [they gently pat Padmé’s shoulders.]

Padmé: Anyway, the other betters quickly followed my lead, so Quark
lost a lot of money that day. I felt sorry for him so I volunteered
to offset his bet as a gift. Then people on social media and mainstream
media accused me of being addicted to gambling - I was not, but I
decided to avoid that just to please them.

Worf: You should not care <b>that much</b> what other people say about you.

Padmé: maybe…
